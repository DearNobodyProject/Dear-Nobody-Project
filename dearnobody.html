About

Dear Nobody is a place where your feelings are free to exist. It's for those moments when you need to share your heart, but can't find the words. Whether it's the pain of a broken heart, the weight of regret, or the relief of closure, your story matters. We take your words and turn them into something real, something that speaks to the heart and connects with others who feel the same. Here, your voice is heard, and it means everything.

How It Works

1. You send your story (real or fictional) to us via WhatsApp.
2. We turn it into a short video story — text-based, with sound and visuals.
3. Your identity stays hidden. Always.
